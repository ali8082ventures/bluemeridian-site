Questions

The fine print, in plain English

What exactly do I earn on — revenue or profit?

Your tier is set by the gross charter revenue you introduce, but your commission is a percentage of Blue Meridian's profit on each deal. Example: you refer a €100,000 charter and Blue Meridian's profit on it is €15,000. At the Commodore tier (15%), you earn €2,250 on that single booking.

When do tiers reset?

Tiers are measured over a rolling 12-month window. Once you qualify for a higher tier, the new rate applies to all deals completing from that point forward.

How does the 1% network override work?

When an ambassador you introduced closes a deal, you earn 1% of Blue Meridian's profit on that deal — on top of their own commission, not deducted from it. It applies to every deal they ever close, with no cap on how many ambassadors you can introduce.

How and when am I paid?

Monthly. Completed charters from the previous month appear on your statement on the 1st, and payment is released by the 7th — by bank transfer (Faster Payments, SEPA or SWIFT) in GBP, EUR or USD.

What counts as "my" referral?

Any client who books through your referral link, or whom you introduce by name to your Blue Meridian contact before their first enquiry. Referred clients remain tagged to you for repeat bookings for 24 months.

Do I need any licence or qualification?

No. Blue Meridian is the broker of record on every charter and handles contracts, payments and compliance. You're rewarded purely for the introduction.

Is there a cost to join?

None. There are no joining fees, subscriptions or sales quotas. If you never refer a client, you simply never earn.
